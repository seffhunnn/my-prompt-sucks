Handcrafted Architecture

A Developer-First Extension

Built for developers who value keyboard navigation, speed, and privacy. Zero tracking. Zero bloated libraries.

Privacy First

Your API Key. Your Storage.

Your Gemini API key is stored safely within Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). We do not host intermediate servers. Your prompts are dispatched directly from your browser to the Google Gemini endpoint.

  • No external analytics or click tracking
  • Zero database dependencies or user logs
  • Source-visible transparency on GitHub

# Role: Frontend Architect...

Most of us type things like "make login page", "write article funny", or "explain quantum physics" into active input boxes, and then wonder why the AI responds with generic, mediocre garbage.

The problem usually isn’t the model. It’s the prompt structure. Modern LLMs are incredibly capable, but they require boundaries, clear roles, context, and detailed requirements to output high-performance answers.

Writing structured system prompts manually for every minor query is tedious. My Prompt Sucks was built to quietly fix that workflow. By pressing a single shortcut, your rough drafts are instantly restructured into professional, optimized instructions inline.

It acts as a silent, intelligent layer between raw human thought and AI execution.
